Common issues with site links are often due to one of the following:
  • If you don't see your web resource's content in the browser window, the URL may not be correct, or the content's server may be down or slow to respond.
  • If your web site opens in a new browser window, it's because the URL has an "http" address. Only pages with an "https" address will open within Etudes in most browsers.
  • If you can't connect to your publisher's website, you may have not entered a valid URL, key and secret. Please contact your Distance Education office for information.
Before saving, make sure all links have a title and a valid URL.
